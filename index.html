(STREAMING VIA IPTV PLAYER)

  • IF YOU ENCOUNTER ANY TECHNICALITIES, PLEASE CLICK ON ‘UPDATE PLAYLIST’.
    1. DOWNLOAD AN IPTV PLAYER APP.
    2. CLICK ON SETTING (⏱️) , CLICK ON PLAYLIST , CLICK PLUS (+) , CLICK ‘NEW M3U PLAYLIST’.
    3. PROVIDE A NAME & FOR M3U/M3U8.
    4. COPY AND FILL THE LINK URL ON YOUR IPTV PLAYER.
    5. CLICK ON SAVE (✓) AND RETURN TO MAIN MENU. HAPPY WATCHING.
